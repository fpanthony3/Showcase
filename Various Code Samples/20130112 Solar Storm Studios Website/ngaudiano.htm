    • I grew up in Hazleton, Pennsylvania where I graduated from high school in 2000. Always an honor student and band geek. After high school I attended East Stroudsburg University for music. After a year of that I decided I didn't want to be a music teacher so I dropped out. I took a little time off, then I went back to school for electronics engineering technology at Lincoln Technical Institute.

    After I finished my education I didn't know what to do. I had a couple of boring jobs, nothing to do with electronics or music. Bar tended for a little bit. Finally I got a job in electronics where I still am today.

    Ever since I was a little kid I was interested in music. My grandmother had an old Hammond organ that I fooled around with every time I was there. I started playing instruments at a young age. I took piano and organ lessons. I had trumpet all throughout my school years. I also did chorus. Junior high was when I really got interested in playing every instrument I could get my hands on. I learned baritone, tuba, french horn, and cornet, messed around with the violin, learned the reeds and flutes, and started learning percussion. I learned everything I possibly could about music theory and started listening to every type of music there was. This is also the time when I started messing around with the guitar and bass. I got to high school and took some music technology courses for learning the recording arts. I've played keyboards and bass in a few bands. We even recorded a demo. Also messed around and made a few custom guitar pedals and a Theremin. In the end the band thing didn't work out. I got married and started a family. I didn't really do much in music for a few years until this whole crazy company thing was mentioned. This was probably the best chance I was going to have to get back to my music and my creative side. They asked me to be CMO of Solar Storm Studios, LLC. I accepted and this where I am. I hope I could make the whole experience you have an enjoyable one with the music. Maybe inspire a few people along the way. Thank you.

    Nick Gaudiano
    Solar Storm Studios
    Chief Media Officer
