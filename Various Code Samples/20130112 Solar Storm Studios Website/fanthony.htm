    • I spent my school ages in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania where I graduated high school in 1990. Miraculously on time. I did not excel in school or appreciate its value until May of 1993 when I re-entered the education system. I then graduated Lincoln Technical Institute with an Associate in Specialized Technology, which is just a fancy description for electronics, 15 months later in August of 1994. I also attended Albright College some years later in 2003 in pursuit of a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems. Life took an interesting turn and I couldn't finish, however I never stop educating myself. The internet is a wonderful resource.

    After I obtained my Associate degree I have had the pleasure of working for some top notch cutting edge technology companies. Just to name a couple, Comwave designed, developed, and manufactured transmitters, amplifiers, and modulator modules for cable television. They got bought by Comcast who was only interested in the product. I was then employed at Lucent Technologies where I worked on the Laser 2000 line products of the Optoelectronics division, which is to say I built fiber optic lasers that make the internet so fast. They spun us off to become Agere Systems and Agere was eventually sold to Tri-quint. Are you noticing a pattern? Me too.

    Since I can remember I have always driven by my creative side. I have all kinds of interesting hobbies. I've done woodwork, leatherwork, music, and a strange variety of other things. As I would be involved in the creative process I always wondered what people would think of my creation. Would they enjoy them as much as I enjoyed creating them? That thought always drove me to want my own business. I have had several side line business since I've been 12 years old, Yes all legal :-)! Some were moderately successful, nothing I could live on, and some failures. In the end they didn't quite work out. Maybe they weren't quite the business I wanted or I reached a point where my own personal resources ran out and I had to make a decision. The I met the group of guys known today as Solar Storm Studios, llc. I knew that this was my one best chance I could ever ask for, the right mix of talent, motivation, and personality drew me to create the business I always wanted. And now thanks to them and you, the fans, I am CEO of Solar Storm Studios, llc. Thank you for your patronage.

    Frederic Anthony III
