    Paulownia Lumber is a sustainable and renewable lumber source. It is a lightweight wood that offers structural integrity with pronounced graining for a beautiful finish. A sturdy wood with resistance to warping, rotting, checking, and craking making it a must have for projects big and small. For centuries the wood has been used for furniture in Japan. It is currently being used for everything from surf boards to musical instruments. Learn More...



    Paulownia Hybrid is a Paulownia tree hybrid with the American Black Locust tree using a patented DNA process. It can grow up to fifty feet in just three years. It does not flower or seed so it is non-invasive. It survives in harsh conditions and can even absorb contaminants, making it a perfect tree for your soil remediation project. The tree is currently being used for soil remediation, carbon sequestering, lumber, and bio-mass/bio-fuel.
