We currently provide for several markets such as lumbering, soil remediation, and reforestation programs. Keep reading to learn more.

Through our Paulownia suppliers we offer quality Paulownia lumber. Paulownia is extremely fast growing and once harvested re-grows from its own roots. Making it a perfect sustainable source of renewable lumber. We can provide test data and samples. Our partnership with Emerald Energy also creates a unique opportunity with the Megaflora, a hybrid of the Paulownia tree. Retaining the qualities of the fast growth rate and re-growing from its own roots, the Megaflora offers yet a harder wood for lumber. Its ability to survive the toughest conditions allows it to be used for soil remediation and reforestation programs worldwide.
