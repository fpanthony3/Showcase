    • I was born and raised in the Great Pacific Northwest, North of Seattle. My father introduced Greg, my twin, and I to computers at a very young age. When I say introduced to computers I don’t mean the “here is how to play games…” variety. I was coding in atari basic around the age of 6 or 7. Not very well, but hey I was kid.

    I graduated High school in 2002. My senior project was an encrypted chat program in JAVA. Since college was expensive and I didn’t have any credit to speak of, I joined the Army to pay for it. I served 4 years active duty with two tours in Iraq and a home station out of Germany. When I got out of the military, there was no one willing to hire an Army veteran in Seattle with my skill set. Greg was already living out here in PA when I left the Army, so I went out to visit.

    I eventually got a job doing exactly what I did in the Army. Not to be one to feel satisfied with a good job and good pay, I immediately began working on a degree completely unrelated to my current job. Currently I am working on a degree in biochemistry at East Stroudsburg University and planning on pursuing a graduate degree in bioinformatics. One night at work we came up with this idea for a company and here I am.

    David Lee
    Solar Storm Studios
